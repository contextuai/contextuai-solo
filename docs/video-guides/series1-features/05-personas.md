# Video 5: Personas — Your AI Personalities

**Duration:** 2 minutes
**Goal:** Show how personas customize AI behavior in chat and connect to external services.

---

## Opening (0:00 - 0:10)

**Voiceover:**
> "Personas let you create specialized AI personalities — from a simple custom chatbot to a database connector that answers questions about your data in plain English."

---

## Scene 1: The 10 Persona Types (0:10 - 0:35)

**On screen:** Personas page → Click "Create Persona" → Step 1 type grid

**Steps:**
1. Show the type selection grid with all 10 types
2. Highlight three categories:
   - **Nexus Agent** — custom personality (general purpose)
   - **Database connectors** — PostgreSQL, MySQL, MSSQL, Snowflake, MongoDB
   - **Integration types** — Web Researcher, MCP Server, API Connector, File Operations

**Voiceover:**
> "There are 10 persona types. Nexus Agent is your general-purpose custom AI personality. The database connectors let you talk to your databases in English. And integration types connect to APIs, MCP servers, and local files."

---

## Scene 2: Creating a Custom Persona (0:35 - 1:15)

**On screen:** Create a Nexus Agent persona

**Steps:**
1. Click **Nexus Agent** card
2. Step 2 appears — fill in:
   - Name: "Brand Copywriter"
   - Category: Creative
   - Description: "Writes marketing copy in our brand voice"
   - System Prompt: "You are a senior copywriter for a B2B SaaS company. Write in a confident, clear, jargon-free tone. Always lead with the benefit, not the feature. Keep sentences short."
3. Click **Create**
4. Show the persona card in the grid

**Voiceover:**
> "Let's create a Brand Copywriter. Pick Nexus Agent, give it a name, and write a system prompt that defines the personality. Be specific — tone, style, rules. The more detail you give, the more consistent it'll be."

---

## Scene 3: Using a Persona in Chat (1:15 - 1:40)

**On screen:** Chat page → persona dropdown

**Steps:**
1. Open Chat → click persona dropdown → select "Brand Copywriter"
2. Ask: "Write a homepage headline for our project management tool"
3. Show the response matching the brand voice defined in the prompt
4. Switch to "None (default)" → ask the same question → show the generic response

**Voiceover:**
> "In Chat, select your persona from the dropdown. Now every response follows those instructions. Compare it to the default — night and day. Your brand copywriter writes like your brand."

---

## Scene 4: Database Persona (1:40 - 2:00)

**On screen:** Quick demo of a PostgreSQL persona

**Steps:**
1. Show creating a PostgreSQL persona with connection credentials
2. Click **Test Connection** — show success with database version
3. In Chat, select the persona and ask: "What were our top 5 customers by revenue last quarter?"

**Voiceover:**
> "Database personas are powerful — connect your PostgreSQL, MySQL, or Snowflake, test the connection, and then ask business questions in plain English. The AI translates to SQL behind the scenes. All your data stays local."

**End card:** "Next: Blueprints — Workflow Templates"
