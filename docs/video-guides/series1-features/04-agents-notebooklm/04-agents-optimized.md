# Video 4: Meet Your Agent Library

> **Director's Context:** ContextuAI Solo ships with 93 pre-built AI business agents — each with a specialized system prompt, recommended model, and tool configuration. Think of them as expert employees you can hire instantly. This is the biggest value proposition of Solo for business users. The audience needs to understand that agents aren't just chat presets — they're specialized AI workers used in Crews and Workspace projects.

**Duration:** 3 minutes
**Goal:** Show users the 93-agent library, how to browse/filter, understand agent details, and create custom agents.

---

## Opening (0:00 - 0:15)

**On screen:** Agent Library page with category filters visible
![Screenshot: The Agent Library showing a grid of agent cards organized by business category with search and filter options.](scene1-agent-library.png)

**Voiceover:**
> "Solo comes with 93 AI business agents — ready to work out of the box. Marketing strategists, financial analysts, legal advisors, HR specialists, data scientists — an entire company's worth of expertise. Let's find the right ones for you."

---

## Scene 1: Browsing by Category (0:15 - 0:50)

**On screen:** Click through category filters — C-Suite, Marketing & Sales, Finance & Operations, etc.

**Voiceover:**
> "Agents are organized into 13 business categories. C-Suite gives you a CEO Strategic Advisor, CMO, CFO, CTO. Marketing and Sales has copywriters, SEO specialists, social media managers. Finance and Operations covers financial analysts, risk managers, operations directors. Click any category to filter. Use the search bar if you know what you're looking for."

**Key point for NotebookLM:** List the 13 categories clearly — this is a selling point. Users should feel the breadth of coverage: C-Suite, Marketing & Sales, Finance & Operations, HR & People, Legal & Compliance, Product Management, Data Analytics, IT Security, Design & UX, Social Engagement, Startup & Venture, Specialized, Creative.

---

## Scene 2: Agent Details (0:50 - 1:25)

**On screen:** Click on an agent card → expand details showing system prompt, model, tools

**Voiceover:**
> "Click any agent to see what makes it tick. Each agent has a specialized system prompt — that's the instructions that make a Copywriter write differently from a Financial Analyst. You'll also see the recommended model and any tools the agent can use. Everything is transparent — no black boxes."

---

## Scene 3: Where Agents Work (1:25 - 1:50)

**On screen:** Show arrows: Agent Library → Crews, Agent Library → Workspace

**Voiceover:**
> "Agents aren't just for browsing. They power two major features. In Crews, you assemble a team of agents that work together — like a Content Strategist, Copywriter, and SEO Specialist collaborating on a blog post. In Workspace, agents run analysis on your projects. We'll cover both in later videos."

---

## Scene 4: Create Your Own Agent (1:50 - 2:35)

**On screen:** Click "Create Agent" → fill in name, system prompt, model selection
![Screenshot: The Create Agent form showing fields for agent name, description, system prompt, and model selection.](scene2-create-agent.png)

**Voiceover:**
> "The 93 built-in agents are just the starting point. Click 'Create Agent' to build your own. Give it a name — say 'Product Launch Specialist'. Write a system prompt that defines its expertise, tone, and focus areas. Pick a model. That's it — your custom agent appears alongside the built-in ones and works everywhere they do. Crews, Workspace, all of it."

**Key point for NotebookLM:** Emphasize that custom agents are first-class citizens — they work exactly like built-in agents. No limitations. This is how users tailor Solo to their specific business.

---

## Closing (2:35 - 3:00)

**Voiceover:**
> "93 agents, 13 categories, and you can build your own. That's the Agent Library — your on-demand team of business experts. Next up: Personas."

**End card:** "Next: Personas — Your AI Personalities" + Subscribe/Follow CTA
