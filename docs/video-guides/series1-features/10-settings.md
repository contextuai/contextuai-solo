# Video 10: Settings & Brand Voice

**Duration:** 2 minutes
**Goal:** Quick tour of settings — API keys, brand voice, appearance, data export.

---

## Opening (0:00 - 0:10)

**Voiceover:**
> "Settings is where you configure Solo to match your business and preferences. The most important feature here? Brand Voice — it shapes how every AI response sounds."

---

## Scene 1: Brand Voice (0:10 - 0:50)

**On screen:** Settings → Brand Voice tab

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to Settings → Brand Voice tab
2. Fill in:
   - Business Name: "Apex Consulting"
   - Industry: Consulting
   - Brand Description: "We help mid-market B2B companies optimize their go-to-market strategy"
   - Target Audience: "VP of Marketing and CROs at companies with 50-500 employees"
   - Content Topics: "GTM strategy, revenue operations, demand generation, sales enablement"
3. Show the **Brand Voice Preview** updating dynamically as you type
4. Click **Save Brand Voice**

**Voiceover:**
> "Brand Voice is the most underrated feature. Tell Solo about your business — name, industry, who you serve, what you talk about. Watch the preview update as you type. Once saved, every response across Chat, Crews, and Workspace will reflect your brand's tone and context."

---

## Scene 2: Appearance (0:50 - 1:15)

**On screen:** Settings → Appearance tab

**Steps:**
1. Show three theme options: Light, Dark, System
2. Click **Dark** — show the app switching instantly
3. Show font size options: Small, Medium, Large
4. Click **Large** — show text scaling up

**Voiceover:**
> "Dark mode for late nights, light mode for client calls. Pick your font size — large is great on big monitors, small packs more on screen. Changes apply instantly."

---

## Scene 3: Data Management (1:15 - 1:40)

**On screen:** Settings → Data & Export tab

**Steps:**
1. Click **Export Data** — show the JSON file downloading
2. Show the filename with timestamp
3. Show the **Clear All Data** button — click it, show the confirmation dialog, click Cancel

**Voiceover:**
> "Export your data anytime — conversations, agents, crews, everything downloads as a JSON file. Use it as a backup or to migrate. Clear All Data is the nuclear option — it wipes everything. There's a confirmation dialog to prevent accidents."

---

3. Show the tech stack links

**Voiceover:**
