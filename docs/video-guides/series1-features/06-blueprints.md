# Video 6: Blueprints — Workflow Templates

**Duration:** 2 minutes
**Goal:** Show how blueprints provide ready-made workflows for crews and workspace projects.

---

## Opening (0:00 - 0:10)

**Voiceover:**
> "Blueprints are workflow templates that give your projects and crews a head start. Instead of writing instructions from scratch, pick a template and customize it."

---

## Scene 1: Browsing Blueprints (0:10 - 0:40)

**On screen:** Blueprints page

**Steps:**
1. Show the blueprint grid — 10 pre-built templates
2. Point out card elements: category badge (Strategy, Content, Marketing), name, description, tags
3. Click category filter → "Marketing" → show filtered results
4. Click source filter → "Library" vs "Custom"
5. Search for "competitive" → show results

**Voiceover:**
> "Solo ships with 10 blueprints across strategy, content, marketing, product, and research. Each one is a proven workflow template. Filter by category, source, or search by keyword."

---

## Scene 2: Using a Blueprint (0:40 - 1:15)

**On screen:** Crew Builder → Step 1 → "Use Blueprint"

**Steps:**
1. Go to Crews → Create Crew
2. In Step 1, click **"Use Blueprint"**
3. Show the blueprint selector appearing
4. Select "Content Marketing Pipeline" blueprint
5. Show the description auto-filling with the workflow steps
6. Continue through the wizard

**Voiceover:**
> "Blueprints shine when creating crews. Click 'Use Blueprint' in the crew wizard, pick a template, and it fills in the workflow description automatically. You get a structured, proven approach instead of starting from a blank page."

---

## Scene 3: Creating Your Own Blueprint (1:15 - 1:50)

**On screen:** Click "Create Blueprint" → fill form

**Steps:**
1. Click **Create Blueprint**
2. Fill in:
   - Name: "Monthly Client Report"
   - Category: Strategy
   - Tags: "reporting, clients, monthly"
   - Content (Markdown): Show typing a structured workflow with steps
3. Click **Create**
4. Show the new blueprint card with "Custom" source badge

**Voiceover:**
> "Create your own blueprints for recurring workflows. Write the steps in Markdown, add tags for easy searching, and save. Next time you create a crew for monthly reporting, your template is one click away."

---

## Closing (1:50 - 2:00)

**Voiceover:**
> "Blueprints save you from reinventing the wheel every time you start a project. Build your own library of workflows as you go. Next — let's put agents and blueprints together in Crews."

**End card:** "Next: Crews — Multi-Agent Teams"
