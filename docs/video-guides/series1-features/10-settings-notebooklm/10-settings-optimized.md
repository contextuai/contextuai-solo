# Video 10: Settings & Brand Voice

> **Director's Context:** This is the final video in the feature series. It covers Solo's Settings page — API key management, Brand Voice configuration, appearance, data export, and version info. Brand Voice is the standout feature: users define their business name, industry, audience, and tone, and every AI response across Chat, Crews, and Workspace is influenced by it. This is what makes Solo feel like YOUR assistant, not a generic chatbot.

**Goal:** Quick tour of settings with focus on Brand Voice as the key differentiator.
