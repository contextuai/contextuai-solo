# Workflow 3: Client Proposal Pipeline

**Duration:** 6 minutes
**Scenario:** A freelance consultant uses Chat for discovery, Workspace for analysis, and a Crew to produce a polished client proposal.
**Flow:** Chat (discovery) → Workspace (analysis) → Crew (proposal document)

---

## Opening (0:00 - 0:20)

**Voiceover:**
> "A potential client just asked for a proposal. You need to research their industry, analyze the opportunity, and deliver a polished document — by tomorrow. Let's chain Chat, Workspace, and Crews to build a proposal pipeline."

---

## Act 1: Discovery in Chat (0:20 - 1:40)

**On screen:** Chat page with a persona selected

**Steps:**
1. Select persona: **"Nexus Agent"** (or a custom "Business Development" persona)
2. First message: "I'm preparing a proposal for a mid-size e-commerce company that wants to improve their customer retention. They have 200K customers, 35% annual churn, and use Shopify. What are the key areas I should investigate?"
3. Show streaming response with structured research areas
4. Follow-up: "What retention benchmarks should I use for e-commerce companies this size?"
5. Show response with industry data points
6. Follow-up: "Draft a 5-point scope of work for a 3-month retention optimization engagement"
7. Copy the scope of work from the response

**Voiceover:**
> "I start in Chat for quick discovery. With a business persona, I ask about key areas for customer retention in e-commerce. The AI gives structured research areas. I follow up for benchmarks and then draft a scope of work. This is brainstorming — fast, conversational, iterative. Now I copy the scope as input for deeper analysis."

---

## Act 2: Deep Analysis in Workspace (1:40 - 3:20)

**On screen:** Workspace → New Project

**Steps:**
1. Click **New Project**
2. Name: "E-Commerce Retention Analysis — Proposal Research"
3. Description: Paste the scope of work, plus: "Produce a detailed analysis covering: 1) Industry benchmarks for e-commerce retention, 2) Root cause analysis of 35% churn rate, 3) Recommended intervention strategies with expected ROI, 4) 3-month implementation timeline with milestones"
4. Select agents:
   - **Data Analyst** — structures the quantitative analysis
   - **Customer Experience Architect** — retention strategy expertise
   - **Financial Analyst** — ROI projections and pricing
5. Create → Execute
6. Show results:
   - **Discussion tab:** Each agent's deep-dive analysis
   - **Compiled Output:** Comprehensive research document with benchmarks, strategies, ROI estimates

**Voiceover:**
> "Now I move to Workspace for the heavy lifting. I paste my scope and assign three specialists — a Data Analyst for the numbers, a Customer Experience Architect for retention strategy, and a Financial Analyst for ROI. Each agent does their own deep analysis, and the compiled output is a comprehensive research document."

**Key moment:** Copy compiled output

---

## Act 3: Polish with a Crew (3:20 - 5:00)

**On screen:** Crews → Create Crew

**Steps:**
1. Create crew: "Client Proposal Generator"
2. Description: Paste the workspace analysis, plus: "Transform this analysis into a professional client proposal. Include: Executive Summary, Situation Analysis, Proposed Approach (3-month plan), Expected Outcomes with ROI, Investment & Pricing, Team & Timeline, Next Steps. Tone: confident, data-driven, client-focused."
3. Execution mode: **Sequential**
4. Agents:
   - **CEO Strategic Advisor** — frames the executive narrative
   - **Copywriter** — writes compelling proposal language
   - **Contract Specialist** — structures terms and pricing section
5. No connections (this is a document, not a social post)
6. Create → Run
7. Show progress:
   - CEO Strategic Advisor: executive framing (completed)
   - Copywriter: full proposal draft (completed)
   - Contract Specialist: pricing and terms (completed)
8. Show final output — a complete client proposal

**Voiceover:**
> "The crew turns raw analysis into a client-ready proposal. CEO Strategic Advisor frames the executive narrative, Copywriter crafts compelling language, Contract Specialist structures the pricing section. Sequential mode means each one refines what came before. The output is a complete, professional proposal."

---

## Act 4: Final Refinement in Chat (5:00 - 5:40)

**On screen:** Back in Chat

**Steps:**
1. Open a new chat session
2. Paste the proposal output
3. Ask: "Review this proposal and suggest 3 improvements to make it more persuasive"
4. Show suggestions
5. Ask: "Rewrite the Executive Summary to lead with the client's 35% churn problem and our expected 12% reduction"
6. Show polished executive summary

**Voiceover:**
> "One last pass in Chat for personal refinement. I paste the proposal and ask for improvements. Then I get the executive summary rewritten to lead with the client's pain point. This is the human-in-the-loop moment — your judgment, AI's speed."

---
