Open-weight models are changing who can build AI products — and who can use them.

ContextuAI Solo ships with a Model Hub of 35+ GGUF models:
- **Meta's Llama 3.1 8B** — the workhorse
- **DeepSeek R1** (7B to 70B) — reasoning powerhouse
- **Qwen 3.5** (up to 27B) — latest from Alibaba
- **Microsoft's Phi-4 14B** — best small model
- **Mistral Small 22B** — fits on 16GB RAM
- **Google's Gemma 3** — efficient and capable

All running on CPU. No GPU. No cloud. One-click download from HuggingFace.

I wrapped these models in a desktop app with 93+ pre-built business agents so non-technical professionals can use them without touching a terminal. Strategy, marketing, finance, legal, product, research — all accessible to anyone with a laptop.

This is what the open-model ecosystem makes possible: real AI products for real people, running on hardware they already own.
