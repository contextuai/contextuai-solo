The tension between AI capability and data privacy is real — especially for business professionals handling sensitive client data.

I built ContextuAI Solo to resolve that tension: a desktop AI assistant with 93+ business agents that runs 100% locally. No data leaves the user's machine. No telemetry. No cloud sync. No accounts.

For users who need more capability, it also supports Anthropic Claude and AWS Bedrock — so they can make informed choices about when local inference is sufficient and when cloud models are worth the tradeoff.

Built the entire app with Claude Code. It was genuinely like having a senior engineering team. The vision of AI that empowers individual creators is becoming reality.

Open source: https://github.com/contextuai/contextuai-solo
