The best AI products will be the ones users forget are AI.

ContextuAI Solo is a desktop app that happens to have 93+ AI agents and 35+ local models inside. You open it, pick an agent (Financial Analyst, Content Strategist, Legal Reviewer), and start working. No API keys, no setup wizard for inference, no "paste your OpenAI key here."

Under the hood: Tauri v2, React 19, FastAPI sidecar, llama-cpp-python on CPU. ~80MB installed. But the user doesn't care about any of that — they care that their marketing strategy draft is ready in 30 seconds.

Built the whole thing with Claude Code. Solo developer.

Free, open source, local-first: https://github.com/contextuai/contextuai-solo
