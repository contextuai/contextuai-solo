# LinkedIn — Consultants & Freelancers

**Target:** LinkedIn post targeting independent consultants and freelancers

---

**I needed an AI assistant that wouldn't be a liability with client data.**

As a consultant, every AI tool that routes through third-party servers is a risk. Client strategies, financial data, HR documents — none of it should leave my machine.

So I built **ContextuAI Solo** — a desktop AI assistant that runs 100% locally.

**What I use it for daily:**
- Strategy analysis with the C-Suite agents (CEO, CFO, COO, CMO, CTO advisors)
- Marketing campaign planning with the Marketing & Sales agents
- Financial modeling and cash flow analysis
- Content creation and copywriting
- Research synthesis and competitive analysis

It comes with **93+ pre-built business agents** across 13 categories. You can also create your own custom agents with specific system prompts for your niche.

**The best part:** 35+ AI models run on your laptop — no subscriptions, no API costs. DeepSeek R1, Qwen 3.5, Llama 3.1, and more. A regular 16GB laptop handles them fine.

I've open-sourced it because independent professionals shouldn't have to choose between AI productivity and client confidentiality.

GitHub: https://github.com/contextuai/contextuai-solo
